Stereotypes


Stereotypes can be be different forms of discrimination. It could be against people's religion, race, and even gender.

Gender

People are stereotyped by their gender by society telling them what jobs are for men and what jobs are for women. Society makes us believe that men should be the main bread winners and women should stay at home and take care of the house and children.

Religion

The religion that people are a part of has always had a huge role in how they are treated in society. For example people have been treated differently for wearing hijabs or indian women getting made fun for wearing a bindi on their forehead.

Coming from a different race can affect the was society views you. People have been called many different slur which affect them and makes them feel embarrassed about their background.
